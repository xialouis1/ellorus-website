Ellorus is a New York-based cabinet manufacturer specializing in semi-custom and fully custom cabinetry for residential and business projects.

About Ellorus

We work primarily with contractors, designers, builders, and project teams, while also serving individual residential clients where appropriate. Each project is developed around its own dimensions, design requirements, materials, finishes, and functional needs.

Made-to-order projects

Ellorus cabinetry is made to order based on project-specific specifications. Pricing, approvals, production timing, payment terms, delivery arrangements, and any applicable change or cancellation terms are confirmed directly with each customer before production begins.
